SWC - Simple Windows Cleaner

A simple and easy to use program for cleaning and freeing up some space in your computer's hard drive, by deleting temporary and unecessary files.

Features

  • Clean Temporary Files

    Cleans temporary files from all windows folders!

  • Disk Cleanup

    Deletes unecessary files in your disk!

  • Recycle Bin Cleanup

    Deletes Whatever is in your Recycle Bin!
